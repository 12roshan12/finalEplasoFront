Meet Our Teams
Sishir Acharya (M.D El Paso Adventure) I am the Director of El Paso Adventure, an esteemed tourism and hospitality organization. With a Master's degree in Tourism and Hospitality Studies, I have over 12 years of experience leading groups as a professional trekking and tour guide. My expertise extends to various mountaineering disciplines, including basic mountain rescue, rock climbing, first aid, and navigation training. I have successfully conquered challenging peaks like Mt. Ama Dablam, Island Peak, Mera Peak, Yala Peak, Himlung Peak, and Chulu East Peak. Additionally, I have volunteered to discover new rock spots near the Kathmandu Valley, expanding trekking possibilities. Despite being Nepali, I am fluent in English, Spanish, Hindi, French, and other languages, enabling effective communication with a diverse range of clients. I have dedicated a significant portion of my life to accompanying tourists to the Himalayas, gaining invaluable experience and expertise.
